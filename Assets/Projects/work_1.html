Stoop by station

Stoop by is a stand for bicycles, but it is also a bicycle sharing system. It is intended that the station follows the path of the cycling route in the city and consists of a certain number of stations. With the help of it, cyclists could follow the route for easy navigation in each zone and they would use it for a break from cycling, with the possibility of wifi charging of all devices. Another convenience is rent uber by bikes through the system on the display. The stand contains a high-tech secured service system, whose locking mechanism is powered by a solar panel located at the top of the stand.>
